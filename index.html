Aaron Nicolson

I am a Postdoc at CSIRO, stationed at the Australian e-Health Research Centre in Brisbane, Australia. I am also a part of CSIRO's Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence Future Science Platform. My research focus is multimodal deep learning for medical image analysis.

In 2020, I completed my PhD at the Signal Processing Laboratory of Griffith University, under the supervision of Kuldip K. Paliwal. There, I investigated deep learning for robust speech processing with projects such as Deep Xi.
